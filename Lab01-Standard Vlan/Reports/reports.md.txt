# Lab 1: Standard VLAN Segmentation on Cisco 2960

## 🎯 Objective
Configure VLANs on a Cisco 2960 switch to segment network traffic by department:
- VLAN 10: Admin
- VLAN 20: Engineering
- VLAN 30: Marketing

This lab demonstrates how VLANs improve security and traffic management in enterprise networks.

---

## 🛠️ Topology
- **Switch**: Cisco 2960
- **End Devices**:  
  - VLAN 10 → 2 PCs on Fa0/1–4  
  - VLAN 20 → 2 PCs on Fa0/5–13  
  - VLAN 30 → 2 PCs on Fa0/14–20
